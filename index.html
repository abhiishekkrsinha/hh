Empowering Rural Healthcare with Technology

HealthConnect bridges the healthcare gap in rural areas. Access medical advice, track health, and get care anytime - even offline.

Game-Changing Features

Designed specifically for rural communities, our app offers comprehensive healthcare solutions at your fingertips.

Offline Mode with Sync

Input symptoms and logs offline, sync when connected. Never let poor network limit your care.

Local Language Voice I/O

Chatbot with voice recognition in regional languages, perfect for illiterate users and hands-free operation.

Community Health Integration

Local workers assist patients, collect data, and escalate cases seamlessly through the app.

Gamification & Rewards

Earn coins through health quizzes and games, redeem for consultation discounts and wellness programs.

Affordable Subscriptions

Free basic plan to premium - pay only for what you need with flexible payment options.

AI-Powered Dashboard

Personalized insights, remote screenings, and proactive health monitoring powered by AI.

Family Health Management

Manage profiles for entire family, schedule group consultations in rural joint-family setups.

Government Scheme Integration

Direct access to AYUSHMAN Bharat, subsidized medicines, and free diagnostics through app.

Tele-Physiotherapy

Live sessions and AI-guided exercises replace costly hospital visits for rehabilitation.

Mental Health Support

Confidential therapy, support groups, and resources addressing rural mental health stigma.

Emergency SOS

Instant GPS sharing with ambulances and family during emergencies for rapid response.

Medicine Verification

Scan QR codes to ensure genuine medicines, combating counterfeit drugs in rural areas.

About HealthConnect

Born from the needs of rural India, HealthConnect revolutionizes healthcare access. We combine AI, voice technology, and local health worker integration to bring quality care to every village.

Our mission is to eliminate healthcare disparities through innovative technology, ensuring no one is left behind due to geography or connectivity challenges.
